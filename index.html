Steve Jobs

Jobs, S. (n.d.). "Your work is going to fill a large part of your life, and the only way to be truly satisfied is to do what you believe is great work. And the only way to do great work is to love what you do. If you haven't found it yet, keep looking. Don't settle. As with all matters of the heart, you'll know when you find it."

Introduction:

Steve Jobs (1955-2011) was an iconic figure in the world of technology and entrepreneurship. He co-founded Apple Inc., revolutionized the personal computer industry, and played a significant role in shaping modern technology and culture.
